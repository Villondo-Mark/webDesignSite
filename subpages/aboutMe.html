My Design Philosophy

Throughout my years of study and panic, I've come to learn a couple of things that have helped shape who I am today, things that reflect on my design work. For one, the concept of balance has always been one that has existed in my work, even before I was aware of it. This meticulousness has been a constant since I was a lad, but after recognizing it, I decided to go ahead utlilize it as a skill.
For me, balance means several things coming together for a single purpose. One can usually tell when something feels off, but I always try to take it a step further by fixing it down to the pixel to the best of my abilities. Of course, this has caused me no small amount of stress and has also been the bane of many a deadline. However, throughout all of this, I've found that, if one is able to find peace and calm through the chaos of trying to achieve balance, they will be able to see more, feel far more confident, and effectively perform better than they initially believed they could.

It is this exact thing that I try to achieve with all my projects. Peace of the internal space of one's spirit leads to peace of external space with one's surroundings.
